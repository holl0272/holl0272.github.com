There are 2 factors that determine the day you will get your order:


  1. The time it takes us to letter your jerseys and prepare them for shipment
  2. Shipping Time


Our goal is to ship every order as soon as possible. Please understand that the TIME and DAY that you place your order impacts shipping time. We ship from Indiana, and cannot letter jerseys all day and night-though we keep long hours. For example, if you place an order at midnight on Saturday night, we will not be able to begin processing it until Monday.


When you place your order, an estimated shipping date will be displayed.


For example, if you place an order right now, the message would read:

This order should ship on


Please be sure to provide a valid e-mail address and contact phone number. There are times when issues arise and we want to contact you as soon as possible to alert you and minimize any delay.


Also, during checkout, you can enter your zip code in order to compare prices of our various shipping options. Compare the shipping date to your own deadline and budget to choose the best shipping option for you. We understand that special circumstances arise and encourage you to Contact Us and we will do our best to take care of your unique situation, even if it means getting a jersey to you tomorrow.


After placing your order, you can follow its progress by logging on to your Your Account.


The most common shipping choice is UPS Ground, but we offer the following expedited options as well:


Within the United States or Armed Forces Addresses (APO, PPO, FPO)


  • UPS Next Day Air
  • UPS Next Day Air Saver
  • UPS 2nd Day
  • UPS 3rd Day Select

Outside the United States


  • USPS International

The map below illustrates the number of BUSINESS days* necessary to ship an order using the UPS Ground option. Remember, your order may not be shipped the day it is placed -- add 1-2 business days to allow us to create your custom jerseys when estimating the date of delivery.


Here's an example. An order shipped to New York City (dark green) will take 3 business days to arrive if UPS Ground is selected, and order to San Francisco, CA (dark red) will be received 4 business days after it is shipped.


Said another way, if you live in New York City and place an order for jerseys on Monday morning to be shipped via UPS Ground, we will letter the jerseys, ship them by Wednesday and you would receive them the following Monday.




Should you need your jerseys sooner, choose one of the expedited shipping methods.


When completing your order, please DOUBLE CHECK that your BILLING address matches the actual CREDIT CARD billing address, or else your order WILL BE DELAYED.


Any questions? Feel free to Contact Us.


* A Business day is a defined as a day that Federal Banks are open (Monday-Friday except Federal Holidays)
