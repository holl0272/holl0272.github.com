Why should I order from GameWearNow?


GameWearNow and its parent company, the Sports Spot, have been providing custom jerseys to schools, youth leagues, recreational leagues and professional teams for over 50 years.


We know you want custom jerseys...quick. We pride ourselves in the speed of our delivery and the quality of our work. GameWearNow has sold jerseys over the web since 1999. Our goal is to deliver you jerseys in about a week.


We also know that sometimes you don't have a week. At GameWearNow, we will do our best to meet your schedule, even if it means getting you a jersey overnights. Just call our toll-free number (877) 776-6693 and we will do everything possible to get your jerseys to you when you need them.


Other companies require minimum orders...not GameWearNow.


Of course, GameWearnNow offers quantity discounts for team orders. We can fill your order no matter how large it might be...we've been earning the trust of our customers for a half-century.



Do you provide step-by-step instructions?


Yes. We understand that, although ordering custom jerseys over the internet is convenient, it can be a confusing process. We've worked hard to streamline our order process and we hope this section answers any questions that you may have.

Can I use my custom logo?


Yes. Though we offer several lettering selections, we are happy to use your custom logo. There is a one-time set-up fee of $35. Be sure to e-mail us a .gif or .jpeg image your logo during the ordering process.



How is the lettering applied?


Lettering is typically applied via heat press. We have been lettering jerseys for decades. We know what we are doing and are confident that your lettering will remain in tact wash after wash after wash.



Do you offer discounts for large orders?


We offer quantity discounts, as you can see when you view each jersey. However, if you have an especially large or complex order, please call or e-mail us and we can provide you a quote for your specific needs.



Can I change my order once its been placed?


Until your order has been customized, you are welcome to call or e-mail us and change the order. Of course, given that our goal is to send your order as soon as possible, please e-mail us as soon as possible so that we can make the change. Please call our toll-free number (877) 796-6639 or Contact Us.


Once your order is lettered to your specifications, changes cannot be made.



What brand of jerseys do you sell?


We feature brands such as Russell Athletic (jerseys and t-shirts), Don Alleson (jerseys), Hartwell Industries (jerseys) and Martin (jerseys). With GameWearNow, you do not sacrifice quality for speed or value.



What if I don't see the jersey style I want?


Yes. We order direct from our Manufacturers. In some cases we have had relationships with them for 40+years. Sports Spot, our parent company, is a full-service sporting goods store. Please Contact Us with your special requests.



How long have you been in business?


Our parent company, Sports Spot has been in business for 52 years. GameWearNow has been selling custom jerseys online since 1999. We hope to earn your trust, just as we have earned the trust of our customers since 1955.
